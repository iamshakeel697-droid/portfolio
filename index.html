About Me

The person behind the timeline

I'm Shakeel Shahid, a video editor and a BSCS (Bachelor of Science in Computer Science) student. For the past 2 years I've been editing videos professionally — everything from fast-paced YouTube content to clean, story-driven brand videos — while studying computer science alongside it.

That mix matters: my technical background means I understand structure, logic, and detail, and I bring that same precision into the editing room — clean cuts, intentional pacing, and a final product that feels effortless to watch even though a lot of decisions went into it.

I care about the story first. Every edit I take on starts with one question: what is this video supposed to make someone feel or do? Everything else — cuts, color, sound, motion — serves that answer.
